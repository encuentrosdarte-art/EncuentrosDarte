The Experience

Not a show.
A conversation with flamenco.

Laraizdelapureza is something you will not find in any theatre or tablao. As the sun sets over the Guadalquivir, you will board a boat in the company of live flamenco artists — not as a spectator in a seat, but as a guest in their world.

Over 90 minutes on the river, the artists will share the history, soul, and language of flamenco with you directly. Ask questions, listen to impromptu performances, and understand what lies behind every beat and cry. Catering is served on deck as Seville glides past.

🎸
Live Flamenco Performance

Guitar, song, and dance performed intimately on the open deck of the boat as you sail.

🗣
Guided Artist Encounter

A facilitated conversation with the artists — stories, tradition, and the living culture of flamenco.

📖
Didactic Flamenco Classes

Learn the rhythms, forms, and vocabulary of flamenco — an introduction you can carry home.

🍽
Catering on Deck

Light catering served during the cruise, enjoyed against the backdrop of the Seville skyline.
